Hi, I'm Mikola. I love programming.

I'm a software engineer from Lviv, Ukraine. I've been building things on the web for years - small side projects, work stuff, and everything in between.

This Blog

This is where I share things I've learned - usually the hard way. The posts here are tutorials and deep dives into problems I've solved. I write them partly to organize my own thoughts, and partly because I wish these guides existed when I was learning.

Topics range from neural networks to GIS data processing to practical web development patterns. It's not super active, but when I post, I try to make it thorough and useful.
